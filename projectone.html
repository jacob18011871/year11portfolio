overview: a low poly character created in blender, demonstrating all the features of blender.

End Users

My model (lets name him nutsach) is targetted towards teenagers who like to play games, and horror. Oh. Right, its also a bag of walnuts. A spooky bag of walnuts... I left the model up to fate and it told me to make it a bag of walnuts! Anyway, really just targetting the younger side of teenagers which have a pretty specific sense of humor. Specifically men. I dunno if everyone will enjoy a living bag of walnuts. Especially one which is meant to be scary. Still. Those who find this at least mildly entertaining, I think they'll like this..!!

  • Basic bag format
  • Smile! You know you're never fully dressed without one...
